EO Guide for Top 1k

What is EO?
Ownership + captain rate = EO. Captaining a 50% player gives ~90% effective ownership to your rival's stack.

Why it matters
When 70%+ have the same EO captain, GW scores are similar. A 10% EO captain who scores is a massive rank swing.

The rule
EO >70%? Consider a differential captain to gain rank. EO <30%? Match template to protect rank first.

Rank Estimate Explained

How it works
Your projected score (including predicted bonus from live BPS) is compared against the global scoring distribution, estimated from player ownership percentages.

Accuracy
Very close to final rank once most matches are played. Early in a GW (few matches played) the estimate is wider. Bonus predictions may shift slightly until FPL confirms them.

Safety score
The points you need to be above the current GW average. If you exceed it, you get a green arrow. Below it, a red arrow.
